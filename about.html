About FindMe

Launched in 2020, FindMe is a fast growing platform for
tracing lost people. We serve millions of individual seekers
around the world.

Our Mission

As part of the FindMe Group, our mission is to make it easy to find our lost loved ones.

We do this by giving seekers the tools necessary to reach a global
audience for their lost ones, and by helping the communuty find
a platform to report persons they find lost.

Anytime, Anywhere

As a platform, we continue to develop services to help
businesses do more and discover new opportunities.

Whether it's sourcing from your mobile phone or contacting
suppliers in their local language, turn to FindMe for all your
global business needs.
